About Cowork for Closers

Cowork for Closers is the companion application to the book of the same name. It contains 55 meticulously engineered AI prompts designed specifically for real estate professionals using Claude's Cowork mode. Every prompt has been tested in live real estate workflows — from luxury listing descriptions to investment underwriting, from transaction management to authority marketing. This isn't a generic prompt library. Every word references real tasks, real tools, and real outcomes that matter in your business.
